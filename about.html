About the practice

A thoughtful, collaborative approach to therapy.

This page is designed to help the therapist introduce their style, values, and background in a way that feels warm, credible, and easy to personalize.

Sample introduction

I believe therapy works best when it feels safe enough for honesty and structured enough for movement. My role is to offer a space where clients can better understand what they are carrying, find language for difficult patterns, and begin making change at a pace that feels sustainable.

My style is warm, steady, and collaborative. I often draw from cognitive behavioral therapy, compassion-focused work, and attachment-informed perspectives, always adapting the process to the person rather than expecting the person to fit a rigid model.
